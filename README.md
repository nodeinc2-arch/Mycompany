# Node2

Building Canada's next IT layer.

Node2 is a Canadian IT company building AI-integrated web platforms, custom local LLMs, and finance automation for businesses that need software they can trust.

## Services

### AI-integrated web platforms
Build product surfaces, portals, and internal systems with AI woven in from day one instead of added later as a patch.

### Local LLMs and Micro AI
Deploy private, domain-tuned AI workflows that keep sensitive data inside your environment.

### Finance and PropTech systems
Automate payroll, integrations, and operational workflows for teams that need reliability more than hype.

## Featured work

### BuildingSync.app
An upcoming SaaS platform for property management and building operations.

### Payroll labs
Internal tooling for payroll integrations, migration previews, and controlled commit workflows.

### Micro AI
Small, focused automations that reduce manual operations without pushing data into public cloud AI systems.

## Micro AI

Small, dedicated AI agents. Massive business impact. Built on OLLAMA, our Micro AI agents run locally on your infrastructure so your data stays private.

- Save time on repetitive operational work.
- Respond instantly without cloud dependency.
- Keep sensitive information under your control.
- Let automations improve as your process matures.

## Finance and payroll

Security. Accuracy. Canadian compliance. We help organizations implement, optimize, and integrate the right financial systems for their needs.

- Workday and SAP implementation support.
- QuickBooks and Xero setup, integration, and reporting workflows.
